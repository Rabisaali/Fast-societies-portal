About Fast

Welcome to FAST where you walk in thinking you'll have a balanced academic life… and FAST immediately says “not on my watch.” The campus life exists, sure, but only if you're willing to sacrifice sleep, grades, or sanity-sometimes all three. Every week brings new assignments, surprise quizzes, or some plot twist that makes you question all your life choices. But somehow, in all this chaos, FAST becomes that unforgettable chapter you'll laugh about for years-once you recover.
