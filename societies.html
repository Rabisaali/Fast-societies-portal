Google Developers' Student Club (GDSC)

GDSC is for Developer Student Clubs and is powered by Google Developers to help and support the developer community within campuses around the globe. Developer Student Clubs train thousands of student developers globally and work with their communities to solve real-life problems. University students who lead a DSC gain access to Google technology, events, and mentor-ship while training their local community through fun meetups, project-building activities, and global competitions.

FAST Entrepreneurship Society (FES)

The formation of the Entrepreneurship Society will help bridge this gap and provide students with the resources and support they need to succeed as entrepreneurs. It would focus solely on promoting entrepreneurship among students and organize a range of events such as guest speaker talks, networking events, and workshops on topics such as pitching, marketing, and funding. Additionally, this society publishes a monthly newsletter that highlights the latest news, opportunities, and success stories from the world of entrepreneurship. This newsletter will also provide a platform for students to showcase their ventures, receive feedback, and connect with potential collaborators.

Join FES
